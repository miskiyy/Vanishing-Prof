# The Vanishing Professor

A 2D pixel-art mystery adventure game that teaches basic cryptography through investigation and storytelling.

Players take the role of a former research assistant searching for a missing cryptography professor. By exploring locations, collecting evidence, and solving cipher-based puzzles, players uncover the truth behind Project Chronos, a hidden system capable of manipulating and erasing digital information.

---

## Genre

- Mystery Adventure
- Investigation
- Educational Game
- Cryptography Puzzle

---

## Educational Objectives

Players will learn:

- Caesar Cipher
- Morse Code
- Binary Encoding
- Vigenère Cipher
- Substitution Cipher

Rather than presenting cryptography as isolated exercises, each cipher is integrated into the investigation and used to uncover critical evidence.

---

## Story Premise

Professor Arga, a renowned cryptography researcher, disappears without warning.

His office contains strange clues:

- A warm cup of coffee
- A locked laptop
- Cryptic research notes
- References to a forgotten project called **Chronos**

As Arga's former student, players must follow the trail he left behind and uncover a conspiracy hidden within the university.
